Wikisource Reader

The App

The Wikisource Reader App is a community-maintained app that lets you download and read freely-licensed works from Wikisource.

Features

Search books New

Search books in different languages from Wikisource which are ready to be read.

Filter languages New

Filter books in different languages to search them easily.

Download selected books New

Download your selected books in Epub format, so that you can read them in your free time.

Store books in your library New

Store all your selected books which you want to read in your library, so that you can select and read them as you like. You can also delete books too.

Read books New

Read your selected books from the Wikisource app! You can resume reading from the page where you have left last time.

Navigate through chapters New

Search for chapters listed in the book and directly navigate to specific chapters as you need.

Annotate texts. New

Highlight, underline and take notes from selected texts and check them later as you need.

Installation & Usage

Installing and using the app is easy:

  1. Download Wikisource Reader app from the Google Play Store.
  2. Search for books of your interest in your prefered languages.
  3. Download, store and read books directly from the app.
  4. Navigate the book easily by jumping through the chapters.
  5. Highlight, Underline, Annotate or Copy texts as you need
  6. Set background and text colors, font size, page margins etc. compatible with your reading preferences.
  7. Listen to text to speech audio when you are not reading.
